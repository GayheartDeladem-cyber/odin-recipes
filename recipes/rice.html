Rice Recipe

Description

This is also worldwide consumed food with nutritional benefits

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Water
  • cooking oil
  • Cooking pot

Steps

  1. Pour an appreciable amount of water into the cooking pot and heat to boil
  2. Add a small amount of cooking oil to the boiling water in the pot
  3. Add the rice and cover the pot and allow to cook under low heat
  4. A soft rice texture indicates a ready-to-eat rice
