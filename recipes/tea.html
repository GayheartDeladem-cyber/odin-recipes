Tea Recipe

Description

This meal is consumed worldwide with many health benefits.

It can be taken with bread, cake, biscuit or any baked product.

Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Tea
  • Water
  • Kettle
  • Spoon

Steps

  1. Pour some water into the kettle
  2. Heat the water to the temperature you require
  3. Transfer the water into a cup and add the tea
  4. Stir with the spoon to get a homogenous mixture
  5. Your tea is ready to drink!
