Pancake Recipe

Description

A delicious food served as dessert and consumed worldwide.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Oil
  • Sugar
  • Flavor

Steps

  1. Pour some flour into a mixing bowl
  2. Add some eggs
  3. Add some sugar
  4. Add some flavor
  5. Stir to mix well
  6. Add some milk
  7. Mix well and transfer in small amounts into a hot pan with oil
  8. Fry for a while
  9. A ready pancake is brown-ish and can be served with some juice
